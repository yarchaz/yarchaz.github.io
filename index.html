Purpose/Theme

We wanted to use a theme that was easily recognizable and based off of a popular novel, so we chose Sherlock Holmes. We will be able to create our own graphics to use on the Zen Garden site, and hope to learn how to animate the smoke coming out of Sherlock Holmes's pipe this semester. The nav bar will be designed as book spines to go along with our theme. Other graphics that we plan to incorporate into the Zen Garden site to go along with our theme include footsteps and a magnifying glass.
